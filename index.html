About us

Christine Baine, Co-owner

For the past 20 years, hair has been a part of my life. I practically grew up behind the chair. At the age of 13, I received my cosmetology license and started my hair journey. I worked my way through high school and eventually college. I studied Administration of Justice at George Mason University and received a bachelors of science. I specialize in balayage and color melting, but love all types of color! Hair cutting, styling, and men's cutting are a major part of our business as well. I am passionate about good products and providing the best products I can find for my clients. I also love continuing my education to keep current and will continue to do so in the future!

Crystal Schemansky, Co-owner

My passion has always been to make people feel good. I love when my guest is smiling and happy after getting their hair done. I have been doing hair for 20+ years. My family owned a cosmetology school so I have been around the hair industry for a long time. My passion is hair color. I love the marriage of science and art. I have been through extensive color training and was once an educator for J Beverly Hills Colour. With that said, I love continuing my education to be current on all of the techniques to create trending looks such as balayage, color melt, ombre, babylights, highlights, and fashion color. I am committed to providing superb service that is personalized for each guest with a luxurious touch. I only use quality products for hair care and color. You will feel like a million bucks when you leave!
